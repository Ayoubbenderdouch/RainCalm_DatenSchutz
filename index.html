Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04/23/2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the RainCalm App (hereinafter "the App") processes information. Protecting your privacy is important to us, and we want to inform you about the data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data Collection and Use

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical data: Basic device information such as operating system version and device model, necessary for the operation of the app
  • Network access: The app accesses weather data via the Internet
  • Cache data: Temporary storage of weather data for better performance

2.2 Data Not Collected

  • We do not collect personal data
  • We do not store your usage history
  • We do not track user activities
  • We do not create user profiles

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The app communicates exclusively with our API to obtain weather data.

4. Data Security

We implement technical security measures to protect the communication between the app and our server:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Regular security updates

5. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet access: To retrieve current weather data

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as necessary. The current version will always be available in the app.

7. Contact

For any questions regarding data protection, you can contact us as follows:

  • Email: contact@raincalm.app
  • Website: https://raincalm.app/

We strive to respond to all requests within 48 business hours.

8. Legal Basis

This privacy policy has been drafted in accordance with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • App Store requirements

9. User Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

Since we do not collect personal data, these rights do not directly apply to our app, but we want to inform you for transparency.

10. Use by Minors

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can take the necessary measures.
